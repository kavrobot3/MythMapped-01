Who We Are

We’re two students from Warangal — Raeyansh and Kavish — who believe that mythology is more than just stories. It’s a mirror into our shared human past. At MythMapped, we're blending history, geography, storytelling, and culture into one interactive platform where ancient tales meet modern curiosity.

What started as a fun idea between cousins became a passion project. We aren’t historians or developers (yet), just two learners who wanted to bring myths to life on a map and help others explore them in a new way.

About MythMapped

MythMapped is a unique initiative that maps world mythology geographically. From the serpent gods of Mesoamerica to the forest spirits of Eastern Europe and the cosmic battles of India, we wanted to visually represent how these powerful stories are rooted in place.

Our goal is to make myths more interactive and accessible. Using timelines, regional cards, and quizzes, MythMapped isn’t just a website — it’s a journey through the imagination and heritage of humanity.
